About Me

My name is Rehaan, and I’m a 17-year-old student-athlete with a passion for making a difference in my community. As a sprinter in the 100m and 200m events, with times of 10.91 and 22.75 seconds, I’ve learned the value of discipline, perseverance, and hard work. But my ambitions go far beyond the track. I want to become a computer scientist, politician, filmmaker, entrepreneur, and content creator—all with the goal of improving the lives of people in and around Palm Acres.

I’ve always been fascinated by how technology can solve real-world problems. I dream of using computer science to develop innovative solutions, like machines that provide renewable energy, making sustainable living more accessible and affordable. At the same time, my entrepreneurial side drives me to create products that solve everyday challenges, like noise-canceling tools for busy work environments or shared spaces where people can access all streaming services. These ideas are inspired by my desire to make life simpler and better for everyone in my community.

As a politician and filmmaker, I want to inspire people and create change on a broader scale. Through film, I can tell stories that shed light on social issues, challenge outdated mindsets, and spark meaningful conversations. In politics, I see an opportunity to amplify the voices of those who often go unheard and implement policies that help people break free from societal pressures. One of my biggest goals is to connect individuals in my community with mentors and professionals who can guide them toward careers they’re truly passionate about, rather than settling for paths they don’t enjoy.

At the core of everything I do is a desire to connect with others and leave a lasting impact. My ultimate dream is to help Palm Acres and communities like it thrive by fostering innovation, creativity, and a shared sense of purpose.
